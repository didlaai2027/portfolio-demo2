Transforming Enterprises Through Intelligent Data

I am a strategic Data & AI executive with over two decades of experience leading large-scale digital transformation programs across Fortune 500 companies in financial services, retail, healthcare, and technology sectors.

My journey began as a data analyst in 2004, and through relentless curiosity and a passion for innovation, I evolved into a C-suite leader who bridges the gap between technical AI capabilities and real-world business value. I have built AI centers of excellence from the ground up, launched GenAI product suites, and spearheaded cloud-native data platform migrations serving millions of users.

Beyond the technology, I am deeply committed to ethical AI governance, inclusive team cultures, and mentoring the next generation of data professionals. I believe the best AI systems are built by diverse, empowered teams with clear purpose.
